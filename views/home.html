    Music is universal. It's present in every culture since the beginning of human kind. Yet all to often, we don't know what music the person sitting next to us listens to. Genre.me strives to connect people by the music they listen to, in particulary by musical genre. Here at Genre.me we have collected a list of nearly 1,000 popular music genres to select from. From the homepage users can quickly access the top genres that Genre.me users have selected. See a genre you've never heard of before?
